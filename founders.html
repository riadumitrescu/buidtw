Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform how creators connect with their audience. By turning creative work into wearable art, we're building a new way for people to express their identity and share their creative journey.

No matter what type of creator you are - developer, artist, designer, writer, or anything in between - we'll work together to showcase your work in a way that's uniquely you. Every piece is fully customizable, ensuring your hoodie truly represents your creative identity.

The Vision

Build to Wear was born from a simple idea: what if we could wear our creative work? Not just as a logo or a design, but as a true reflection of our creative process and identity.

Problems We're Solving

Buried Work

Most creative work gets buried in portfolios, PDFs, or feeds — it's hard to see people's process in real life.

Generic Merch

Merch feels generic, and personal branding often becomes performative or self-promotional.

Missing Context

Creators rarely get to showcase why they made something — only what they made.

Lost Connection

It's difficult to feel emotionally connected to your own work once it's shipped or archived.

How We Solve It

Wearable Stories

We turn your real creative journey into a custom wearable piece — your hoodie becomes your story.

Creator-First

We center the creator, not the brand — no logos, no fluff — just your work, your voice, your values.

Rich Documentation

Every hoodie includes thoughtful elements: quotes, sketches, QR codes, and a feature page — not just clothing, but documentation.

Intentional Design

We offer a quiet, intentional alternative to performative personal branding — something you can feel proud to wear, not just post.
